Week 1 Focus: 70–80% intensity. All ball work stays middle height, middle of the plate. Build the pattern and groove the swing before adding any challenge. Viz sessions fall on separate days — cage days, off days, or whenever pitchers are throwing.
🛑 Stop Session & Contact PT/MD if any of these occur
  • Sharp or deep anterior shoulder pain during or after swinging
  • Pain > 3/10 at any point
  • New or increased clicking / catching sensation
  • Numbness or tingling down the arm
  • Next-day soreness not resolved within 24 hours

→ Week 4: 90% base + escalating 100% reps · Stand-in becomes free-swing live ABs

Viz sessions shift to live stand-in + free swinging — no restrictions. Full zone, full approach, full competitor.
